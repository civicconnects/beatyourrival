Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2024 | Effective Date: December 15, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Beat Your Rival ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using Beat Your Rival, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you use our App, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, profile picture
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials (managed securely via Firebase Authentication)
  • Profile Information: Display name, bio, performance preferences
  • Payment Information: Subscription details (processed securely via Stripe - we do not store credit card information)

2.2 Content You Create

  • Video Recordings: 90-second performance videos you record within the App
  • Battle Data: Battle challenges, moves, scores, and votes
  • User Interactions: Comments, likes, votes, and activity feed interactions

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, crash reports
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, app errors, performance metrics

2.4 Location Information

We do not collect precise location data. General location may be inferred from your IP address for analytics purposes only.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Provide and Improve Services

  • Enable video recording, storage, and playback
  • Facilitate battle creation, participation, and voting
  • Maintain ELO ranking and leaderboard systems
  • Process subscription payments and manage premium access

3.2 Communication

  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Notify you about battle challenges and results

3.3 Analytics and Performance

  • Analyze app usage patterns to improve features
  • Monitor app performance and fix technical issues
  • Generate anonymized aggregate statistics

3.4 Security and Compliance

  • Detect and prevent fraud, spam, and abuse
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 Public Information

The following information is publicly visible to other App users:

  • Username and profile picture
  • Performance videos you submit to battles
  • Battle scores and rankings
  • Votes and activity feed interactions

4.2 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers:

  • Firebase (Google LLC): Authentication, database, storage, analytics (Privacy Policy)
  • Stripe: Payment processing for subscriptions (Privacy Policy)
  • Cloud Storage: Video storage and content delivery

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with valid legal requests from authorities
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Prevent fraud or security threats

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where We Store Data

  • Firebase Cloud Firestore: Battle data, user profiles, activity feed
  • Firebase Storage: Video recordings
  • Geographic Location: United States (primary), with global CDN for content delivery

5.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest for stored videos
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Auth
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Access controls and least-privilege principles

5.3 Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Video Content: Stored for the duration of associated battles plus 30 days
  • Battle History: Retained for ranking and leaderboard purposes
  • Deleted Accounts: Data permanently deleted within 90 days

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Update

You can access and update your account information at any time through the App settings.

6.2 Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion by:

  • Using the "Delete Account" option in App settings
  • Contacting us at: privacy@beatyourrival.com

6.3 Your Rights (Depending on Location)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

EU/EEA Users (GDPR): Contact privacy@beatyourrival.com to exercise your rights.

California Users (CCPA): Contact privacy@beatyourrival.com to exercise your rights.

7. Children's Privacy

Beat Your Rival is not intended for users under 13 years of age (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete the account immediately and remove all associated data.

If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us at: privacy@beatyourrival.com

8. International Data Transfers

If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy in the App.

We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • In-app notification
  • Email to your registered address
  • Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

10. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, contact us:

Email: privacy@beatyourrival.com

Support: support@beatyourrival.com

Response Time: We aim to respond to all inquiries within 30 days.
